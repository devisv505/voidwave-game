The pitch

VOIDWAVE is a 2D factory-automation survival game. You're a lone operator dropped onto a dead planet with a pod and a hand-drill. Mine ore, smelt it, and lay belts so the work runs itself — then build a wall of turrets and hold the line as the Void surges out of the ground in escalating waves.

The loop is simple and relentless: mine, build, automate, survive the next wave — then do it bigger. Every creature you kill drops a Void Core that powers your tech, so progress literally requires killing more of the thing trying to kill you. The factory grows because the threat grows.

Key features

  • Hands-off belt automation — thousands of items flowing from drills to guns across three belt tiers while you fight.
  • A full production chain — smelters, constructors, assemblers, splitters, mergers and storage turn raw ore into turrets and weapons.
  • Survive the Surge — endless waves of glitch-wireframe "Void" enemies that hit harder the more the rift destabilizes.
  • Harvest-the-enemy progression — a 5-tier tech tree gated behind Void Cores dropped by the waves.
  • A signal-glitch art style — custom shaders, VHS-tearline enemies, and a clear cyan-vs-magenta visual language.
